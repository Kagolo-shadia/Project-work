About Us

Founded in 2025 by the visionary Kagolo Shadia, our restaurant has deep roots in the traditions and tastes of Ugandan cuisine. What began as a humble kitchen serving neighbours and travellers has grown into a culinary haven that honours the essence of Uganda’s rich and diverse food culture. Shadia’s passion for bold, nourishing meals lives on in every dish we serve today.


We specialise in exotic Ugandan dishes that showcase a variety of flavours—from smoky grilled meats and creamy stews to delicately spiced vegetables and savoury banana-based staples. Every meal is a balance of tingling, tantalising tastes designed to awaken your senses and leave a lasting impression.


Our food is not just made to satisfy hunger, but to create joy, warmth, and connection.

More than a restaurant, we are a legacy of flavour and hospitality passed down through generations. Step into our space and experience the comforting aroma of time-honoured recipes, the welcoming spirit of our team, and a celebration of Uganda’s culinary soul. We invite you to join us and enjoy a savoury journey that has been over a century in the making.

Our Mission:

To bring the heart of Uganda’s culinary heritage to your table, one delicious meal at a time.

Our Vision:

To be the leading destination for authentic Ugandan cuisine, where every dish tells a story and every guest feels at home.
